How to Use Optibit

  1. Open Nearby tab and tap Turn On to enable Optibit mode.
  2. Bluetooth: tap Scan and pick a nearby device.
  3. WiFi Direct: switch mode, share room code, then connect.
  4. Open the chat and send text, image, video, voice, or file.

Browser security does not allow a website to force-toggle your phone Bluetooth hardware. If Bluetooth is off at system level, turn it on in device settings, then scan from the app.
